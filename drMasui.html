Dr. Hirokazu Masui

Assistant Professor, Kyushu Institute of Technology.

Received his B.S degree from the Department of mechanical engineering, Kyushu Institute of Technology, Japan, in 2001, and the M.S. and Ph.D degree from the Department of Advanced Energy Engineering Science, Kyushu University, in 2003 and 2006, respectively. From 2006 to 2010, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Laboratory of Space Environment Interaction Engineering (LaSEINE) at Kyushu Institute of Technology. From 2010 to 2013, he was an Assistant Professor in LaSEINE. Since 2014, he has been an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Science for Integrated System Engineering at Kyushu Institute of Technology. His research interests include nano-satellite development, environmental testing, spacecraft charging, and space propulsion.
